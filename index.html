HOUSE OF ERA:

The Creative Cult.

House of Era is a creative studio specialized in brand expression and visual identity based in Mexico City. We create a brand identity, graphic design, art direction & production, as well as web and packaging design. Our distinctive approach reflects the view we currently have of the world around us.

House of Era begins with the idea of creating a new vision and thinking outside of the ordinary. We are our own inspiration and we believe that a new era is yet to come. A creative, innovative and expressive era that inspires people to develop a new way of living, from business to lifestyle.
